连词和状语从句
Conjunctions & Adverbial Clauses

概念理解

状语从句：在句中作状语，修饰主句的动词、形容词或副词，说明时间、地点、原因、条件、目的等。

When I got home, my mother was cooking. 🔊
当我到家时，妈妈正在做饭。（时间状语从句）

时间状语从句

连词 含义 例句
when 当...时 When I arrived, it was raining.
while 当...时（持续动作） While I was reading, he came in.
before 在...之前 Finish your homework before you play.
after 在...之后 After he finished, he went home.
since 自从... I have lived here since I was born.
until / till 直到... Wait here until I come back.
as soon as 一...就... I'll call you as soon as I arrive.
💡 注意
as soon as 引导的从句也遵循"主将从现"原则
As soon as he comes, I will tell him.

条件状语从句

常用 if 引导，表示假设条件

If it rains tomorrow, we will stay at home. 🔊
如果明天下雨，我们就待在家里。
💡 主将从现原则
在条件状语从句中，主句用将来时，从句用一般现在时表将来。

原因状语从句

连词 含义 例句
because 因为（最常用） He didn't go because he was ill.
since 既然（已知原因） Since you are here, let's start.
as 由于（语气较弱） As it was late, we went home.
⚠️ 注意
because 和 so 不能同时使用！
❌ Because it rained, so we stayed home.
✓ Because it rained, we stayed home.
✓ It rained, so we stayed home.

结果状语从句

常用 so...that... 或 such...that... 引导

结构 用法 例句
so + adj/adv + that 如此...以至于... He ran so fast that I couldn't catch him.
such + n + that 如此...以至于... It was such a good book that I read it twice.

目的状语从句

常用 so that 引导，表示"为了"

He studies hard so that he can pass the exam. 🔊
他努力学习是为了通过考试。

让步状语从句

常用 although/though 引导，表示"虽然"

Although it was raining, we went out. 🔊
虽然下雨了，我们还是出去了。
⚠️ 注意
although 和 but 不能同时使用！
❌ Although he was tired, but he kept working.
✓ Although he was tired, he kept working.
✓ He was tired, but he kept working.

📝 本节要点

• 时间：when, while, before, after, since
• 条件：if（主将从现）
• 原因：because, since, as
• 结果：so/such...that
• although 与 but 不能同时用
